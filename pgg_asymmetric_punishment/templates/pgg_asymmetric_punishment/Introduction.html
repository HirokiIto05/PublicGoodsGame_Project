In this experiment, you will repeatedly play a public goods game in a group of {{ C.PLAYERS_PER_GROUP }} participants.

In each round, you receive an endowment of points. You can decide how many points to contribute to the group project and how many to keep for yourself.

The total contributions of all group members are multiplied by {{ C.MULTIPLIER }} and then divided equally among all group members, regardless of their individual contributions.

In some rounds, a punishment system may be available. Depending on the punishment system, group members can punish each other (peer punishment) or punishment may be imposed automatically by a rule (central punishment).

Your earnings in the experiment will depend on your own decisions and the decisions of the other group members.
